1. about:~$ whoami █

So… why AI?

I started out in physics and electrical engineering. I liked understanding how things actually work, from the math down to the circuits. Somewhere along the way I realised the most fascinating systems weren't just the ones I was wiring up, but the ones that could learn from data and adapt to the world around them. So I leaned all the way in and did an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at Università di Bologna.

What I love doing

Taking a messy real-world problem and turning it into something a model can actually help with. Sometimes that's building LLM applications, RAG pipelines, and Agent harness. Other times it's applying machine learning to forecasting, anomaly detection, and data-driven decision systems. What I enjoy most is the translation: taking a complex technical result and explaining it so a non-technical person goes "oh, that's actually useful."

When I'm not coding

I'm probably talking to people, honestly one of my favourite things. I love picking up languages (Arabic, English, Turkish, Italian so far, and always tempted to start another), because every new one is a new set of people you get to really understand. And when I want to switch my brain off, you'll find me at a billiards table lining up the next shot. 🎱
